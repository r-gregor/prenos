Top Choices for Ease of Use

Squarespace:
Often recommended for its clean interface and drag-and-drop editor, 
making it easy for beginners to create professional-looking websites. It 
is especially good for portfolios and small businesses.

Weebly:
Offers a straightforward editing experience that can be easier than 
Wix's, with a simple interface perfect for users looking to build a site 
quickly.
SITE123:
A completely free and exceptionally easy-to-use builder with built-in 
support to help you get started quickly.

Other User-Friendly Options

Webnode:
  A quick and easy solution for building a website without many complex 
design options, suitable for those who want simplicity.

Hostinger Website Builder:
An affordable choice that integrates many AI tools to help speed up the 
website creation process.
GoDaddy Website Builder:
Focuses on getting a site up and running quickly with a simple approach 
to website building.

Considerations When Choosing

  Simplicity vs. Features:
  Platforms like Weebly offer more simplicity but fewer advanced 
features compared to Squarespace or Wix.

Cost:
Some platforms like SITE123 offer generous free plans, while others like 
Squarespace can be more expensive but provide superior premium plans, 
according to Accio.

Specific Goals:
Shopify is best for serious online stores, while Squarespace is ideal 
for portfolios and small businesses.
